About

For over a decade, I've been at the forefront of Africa's financial revolution, working to build inclusive systems that empower communities and unlock economic potential across the continent. As Chief Strategy Officer at Horizon Capital Group, I lead strategic initiatives that bridge traditional finance with innovative solutions, ensuring our work creates lasting impact for millions.

My approach to leadership is rooted in the belief that true strategy isn't just about forecasting market trends—it's about understanding the deeper currents that shape human behavior and economic systems. I've learned that the most transformative strategies emerge when we're willing to look inward first, examining our own assumptions and biases before charting a course forward.

Throughout my career, from advising central banks to restructuring multinational portfolios, I've championed the idea that Africa's financial future won't be built by importing Western models, but by crafting solutions that honor our unique contexts while embracing global best practices. This requires courage, cultural intelligence, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Every challenge I've encountered has reinforced one fundamental truth: sustainable growth requires us to constantly push beyond our perceived limitations, both individually and collectively.

I like to find the horizon within and then go beyond.
